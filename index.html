Our Story

More Than Just a Cafe

Founded in 2024, SKA TEA CAFE is Rautahat's first and only venue that combines a professional skating rink with a premium cafe experience. Located in Chandrapur-05, we've created a space where families, friends, and couples can enjoy something truly unique.

Our covered outdoor rink features string lights and a sound system, while our cafe serves authentic Nepali chai, fast food, and speciality beverages. Whether you're a skating pro or trying it for the first time, we've got you covered with safety gear and friendly staff.
